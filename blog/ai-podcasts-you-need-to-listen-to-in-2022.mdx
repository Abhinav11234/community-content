## Podcast are the way!

If you're interested in staying up-to-date with all things artificial intelligence, you've come to the right place! Here are the top 11 AI podcasts that will keep you informed on the latest news, insights, and developments in the world of AI.

### The Artificial Intelligence Podcast 

[The Artificial Intelligence Podcast](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/the-artificial-intelligence-podcast/id1558470439) is a weekly podcast that covers the latest in AI news and research. Hosts Sam DeBrule and Rob May dive deep into the most important topics in AI, including machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and more.


### AI Weekly Podcast 

[AI Weekly Podcast](https://anchor.fm/lablab-ai) a show where the founders of one of the world's leading AI companies discuss the week's hottest industry news. The podcast also has a section where they talk with top AI leaders, researches and influencers about the impact of technology on people's lives and the future of the planet. This is something you must listen to if you want to be aware of how advanced technology is changing the world!


### Talking Machines 

[Talking Machines](https://www.thetalkingmachines.com/episodes) is a podcast about machine learning, hosted by Katherine Gorman and Ryan Adams. In each episode, Katherine and Ryan interview leading researchers in the field to get their perspectives on the latest developments in machine learning.


### Data Skeptic 

[Data Skeptic](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/data-skeptic/id890348705) is a podcast about data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. In each episode, host Kyle Polich interviews leading experts in the field to get their insights on the latest trends and developments.

### This Week in Machine Learning & AI 

[This Week in Machine Learning & AI](https://twimlai.com/podcast/twimlai/) is a weekly podcast that covers the latest in machine learning and artificial intelligence. In each episode, hosts Sam Charrington and Katherine Gorman interview leading experts in the field to get their insights on the latest news and developments.


### The Data Science Podcast 

[The Data Science Podcast](https://developer.ibm.com/podcasts/the-data-science-podcast/) is a podcast about data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. Hosted by IBM, there are the interviews with leading experts in the field to get their insights on the latest trends and developments.


### Linear Digressions 

[Linear Digressions](http://lineardigressions.com/) is a podcast about data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. In each episode, hosts Katie Malone and Ben Jaffe interview leading experts in the field to get their insights on the latest trends and developments.


### O'Reilly Data Show 

[The O'Reilly Data Show](https://www.oreilly.com/radar/topics/oreilly-data-show-podcast/) is a podcast about data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. In each episode, host Roger Magoulas interviews leading experts in the field to get their insights on the latest trends and developments.

### DataFramed 

[DataFramed](https://www.datacamp.com/podcast) is a podcast about data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. In each episode, host Hugo Bowne-Anderson interviews leading experts in the field to get their insights on the latest trends and developments.


### Partially Derivative 

[Partially Derivative](http://partiallyderivative.com/podcast/) is a podcast about data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. In each episode, hosts Chris Albon and Jonathon Morgan interview leading experts in the field to get their insights on the latest trends and developments.


### Machine Learning Guide 

[The Machine Learning Guide](https://ocdevel.com/mlg) is a podcast about machine learning, hosted by Yannic Kirk. In each episode, Yannic interviews leading researchers in the field to get their perspectives on the latest developments in machine learning.

That's a wrap for our list of the 11 best AI podcasts you need to listen to in 2022! We hope you found a new favorite (or two) and that you're ready to dive deeper into the world of AI.
